Services

Musculoskeletal Rehabilitation
Specialized rehabilitation focusing on the musculoskeletal system, aiding in recovery from injuries or surgeries related to bones, muscles, ligaments, and tendons.
Stroke Rehabilitation
Tailored rehabilitation programs for individuals recovering from strokes, aiming to restore mobility, speech, and cognitive functions.
Cardiovascular and Respiratory Rehabilitation
Rehabilitation plans designed to improve heart and lung health, often after cardiac surgeries, heart attacks, or respiratory conditions.
Geriatric Rehabilitation
Rehabilitation services for elderly individuals to address age-related conditions, improve mobility, and enhance quality of life.
Obesity and Fitness
Guidance and programs focusing on weight management, fitness training, and lifestyle changes to combat obesity and improve overall health.
Post Operative Physiotherapy and Rehabilitation
Tailored rehabilitation plans post-surgery to aid in recovery, restore function, and prevent complications.
Ergonomic Training
Education and training on ergonomic principles to prevent workplace injuries and improve posture and comfort.

Therapy

Manual Mobilisation
Hands-on therapy techniques to improve joint mobility, reduce pain, and restore function.
Soft Tissue Release
Techniques to release tension and improve flexibility in muscles, tendons, and fascia.
Postural Training
Exercises and education to correct and maintain proper posture, reducing strain on the body.
Neurodynamics
Techniques to assess and treat nerve mobility and function, often used for nerve-related pain.
Cupping Therapy
Ancient therapy using suction cups to improve blood flow, reduce inflammation, and promote healing.
Ultrasound Therapy
Therapeutic ultrasound waves used to stimulate tissue repair, reduce inflammation, and relieve pain.
TENS - Trans Electrical Nerve Stimulation
Electrical stimulation therapy to relieve pain by blocking pain signals and promoting endorphin release.
Electrical Muscle Stimulation
Use of electrical impulses to activate muscle contractions, aiding in muscle strengthening and rehabilitation.
Muscle Energy Technique
Manual therapy technique involving active and passive muscle contractions to improve joint function and reduce pain.
Foam Roller Exercises
Exercises using a foam roller to improve flexibility, release muscle tension, and prevent injuries.
Proprioception and Balance Exercises
Exercises to enhance proprioception (awareness of body position) and balance, crucial for injury prevention and rehabilitation.
